# MechMind OS v10 - Pricing Model

## Overview

MechMind OS uses a **3-tier SaaS pricing model** designed to capture value across different shop sizes while maintaining a target LTV/CAC ratio of 3:1+.

**Target ARPA (Average Revenue Per Account):** €82/month (blended across tiers)

## Pricing Psychology & Positioning

### Anchor Strategy
- Pro tier positioned as "Most Popular" to drive majority selection
- 3x price jump to Enterprise creates clear value distinction
- Starter acts as trial/acquisition tier

### Value Metrics
- **Per-booking cost:** Starter €0.98, Pro €0.20, Enterprise €0.06 (at capacity)
- **Per-mechanic cost:** Starter €49, Pro €33, Enterprise €<10 (at scale)

| Competitor | Their Price | Our Advantage |
|------------|-------------|---------------|
| Generic booking SaaS | €30-50 | AI voice assistant included |

| Legacy DMS | €500+ | Cloud-native, no hardware |

## Pricing Governance

### Review Cadence
- **Quarterly:** Competitive analysis
- **Bi-annually:** Customer feedback review
- **Annually:** Full pricing strategy review

### Approval Process
| Change Type | Approver | Notice Required |
|-------------|----------|-----------------|
| Promotional discount | Sales Lead | None |
| Standard pricing | C-level | 90 days |
| Add-on pricing | Product Lead | 30 days |

### Grandfathering Policy
- Existing customers keep their pricing for 12 months after increase
- Annual subscribers locked until renewal
- Downgrades subject to new pricing

## Billing & Payments

### Billing Cycles
- Monthly: Charged on signup date
- Annual: Charged upfront on signup date

### Payment Methods
- Credit/Debit cards (Stripe)
- SEPA Direct Debit (EU)
- Bank transfer (Enterprise only, annual only)

### Failed Payment Handling
1. **Day 1:** Retry automatically
2. **Day 3:** Email notification
3. **Day 7:** Final notice
4. **Day 10:** Account suspension (data retained)
5. **Day 30:** Account deletion (GDPR compliant)
